Welcome to the Embassy of Italy in Islamabad

The Embassy of Italy in Islamabad is committed to fostering and maintaining the strong relationship between Italy and Pakistan. We offer a wide range of consular services to Italian citizens and assist Pakistani nationals with their visa needs to visit Italy.

Meeting Between Imran Khan and Italian Chef

In a significant diplomatic dialogue, former Prime Minister Imran Khan met with a renowned Italian chef to discuss visa facilitation for culinary professionals. The focus was on enhancing collaboration and promoting cultural exchange between Pakistan and Italy.

Our Services

Visa Services Offered

The Embassy of Italy in Islamabad offers a variety of visa services tailored to meet the needs of individuals seeking to travel to Italy. Services include assistance with job and working visas, family visas, student visas, and general visa consultations. Our dedicated team is committed to providing support and guidance throughout the visa application process, ensuring a smooth experience for all applicants.




Italy and Pakistan Relations

Italy and Pakistan have enjoyed strong diplomatic relations since the establishment of their official ties in 1948. Over the years, their relationship has flourished, especially in trade, cultural exchange, and education. Italy is a key trading partner of Pakistan within the European Union, importing textiles, leather goods, and agricultural products. In return, Italy exports machinery, automobiles, and high-tech equipment to Pakistan.

Both countries also collaborate in defense and security, sharing expertise and training programs. Additionally, a significant Pakistani diaspora in Italy strengthens the cultural and social connections between the two nations.
